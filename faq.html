📦 Orders & Delivery

How long does delivery take?
Kampala deliveries arrive within 24 hours. Upcountry deliveries take 2-3 business days. Express delivery available within 2 hours in Kampala.
What are the delivery charges?
Free delivery in Kampala for orders above UGX 50,000. Standard delivery fee is UGX 5,000 within Kampala and UGX 10,000 upcountry.
Can I track my order?
Yes! Check your order status in your account dashboard under "My Orders". You'll also receive SMS updates.
What if I'm not home for delivery?
Our delivery agent will call you. You can reschedule or authorize a neighbour to receive the package.

💊 Pharmacy & Medicines

Do I need a prescription?
Over-the-counter (OTC) medicines can be purchased without a prescription. Prescription drugs require a valid prescription from a licensed doctor. You can upload it during checkout.
Are your medicines genuine?
100%. All medicines are sourced from NDA-approved suppliers and pharmacies in Uganda. We guarantee authenticity.
Can I return medicines?
For hygiene and safety reasons, medicines cannot be returned once dispatched. Contact us immediately if you receive damaged goods.

👨‍⚕️ Doctor Consultations

How do consultations work?
Select your consultation type, choose a doctor, pick a time slot, and complete payment. You'll receive a confirmation with a link to join the video/audio call.
Are the doctors licensed?
Yes, all doctors are licensed by the Uganda Medical and Dental Practitioners Council (UMDPC) with active practicing certificates.
Can I get a prescription online?
Yes, our doctors can issue electronic prescriptions during consultations, which you can use to order from our pharmacy.

💳 Payment

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, Visa/Mastercard, and bank transfers. Cash on delivery is available in select areas.
Is my payment secure?
Absolutely. All payments are processed through encrypted, secure channels. We do not store your payment details.
How do promo codes work?
Enter your promo code at checkout. Valid codes include MEDICAL10 for 10% off your order. Terms apply.
