Last Updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

When you use MedicalAssist, we collect information you provide directly: name, email address, phone number, shipping address, payment information, and health-related data needed for consultations or prescriptions.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders and consultations
  • Communicate with you about your account and orders
  • Improve our services and platform experience
  • Comply with legal obligations in Uganda

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure data storage, and access controls. Your health data is treated with the highest confidentiality in compliance with Ugandan data protection laws.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with: licensed healthcare providers (doctors, pharmacies, labs) to fulfill your requests, payment processors for transaction processing, and authorities when required by Ugandan law.

Your Rights

You have the right to: access your personal data, request corrections, delete your account, and withdraw consent for data processing at any time by contacting us.

Cookies

We use necessary cookies for platform functionality. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@medicalassist.ug or call +256 700 123 456.
