❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do subscriptions work?
Choose a plan, select your medications, and we'll deliver them to your door on a regular schedule. You can pause or cancel anytime.
Can I change my subscription plan?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect from your next billing cycle.
Is there a delivery fee?
Free delivery within Kampala for all plans. A small fee applies for upcountry deliveries.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. No cancellation fees. Your subscription continues until the end of the current billing period.
